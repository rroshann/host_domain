Privacy Policy

Product: Servpro QBO Connector
Purpose: Generate internal operational/accounting reports from QuickBooks Online.

Data We Access

  • QuickBooks Online entities (e.g., Customers, Invoices, Accounts) and related metadata.
  • Optional: QuickBooks Time (timesheets) if configured separately.

How We Use Data

To create internal reports, troubleshoot errors (using timestamps and Intuit request IDs), and ensure service reliability. We do not sell or share data with third parties except trusted service providers under confidentiality.

Retention

Raw pulls and logs are retained for a limited period (e.g., 30–90 days) and then deleted or overwritten. Aggregated reports may be stored longer for audit/business purposes.

Security

Transport via TLS; least-privilege access; secrets stored in environment variables or a secret manager; tokens not logged. Access restricted to authorized personnel.

Your Choices

Disconnect the app at any time. For deletion requests or questions, contact support.
